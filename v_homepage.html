About Me

Learn more about my journey, skills, and passion for social work

Vanita Mohan Wathore

Vanita Mohan Wathore is a passionate social worker with over 14 years of experience in the field of community development, women's safety, child welfare, and nutrition. She has worked with renowned organizations such as SNEHA, CCDT, Masuri Manav Vikas Foundation, Sayouh, and Pratham Trust.

She currently runs her own independent consulting and job guidance initiative, helping others grow in the NGO and development sector.
